My Clinic

My Clinic is a modern web application for clinic queueing that can be used by everyone. The existence of my clinic really helps patiens or visitors to take queue without queuing at the receptionist so that it is more effecient, time saving and space saving. This aplication also reduces crowds during the pandemic because the patient does not need to take the queue number at the reception orders.
